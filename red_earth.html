Red Earth & Pouring Rain Review

Written in 1992 this was the surprise book of the month! Sent to me by Arabelle Britton, whom I can't thank enough for introducing me to this book, we take a wonderful journey through Indian culture, both past and present. Let's jump in.

Our narrators, Sanjay, and Abhay, paint two depictions of Indian life. One from the turn of the 20th century, and the other from the late 20th century on American soil. Abhay is thrust back into Indian life after his college years in America. Sanjay is also thrust back into Indian life, but through other means. Let's explore Sandeep a little bit.
